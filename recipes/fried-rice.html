Fried Rice

A Home-Made Fried Rice

A quick fried rice recipe like you get at your favorite Chinese restaurant. Leftover rice, plus a couple of eggs, baby carrots, peas, and soy sauce is all you need. Garnish with sliced green onions, if desired.

  • Baby Carrots : ⅔ cup chopped baby carrots
  • Green Peas : ½ cup frozen green peas
  • Vegatable Oil : 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Eggs : 2 large eggs
  • Rice : 3 cups leftover cooked and chilled white rice
